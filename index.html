I am currently a Researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab.

Before, I obtained the Master degree at 2020 from National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received the B.Eng degree in Automation at 2017 from the Northeastern University with Honors.
