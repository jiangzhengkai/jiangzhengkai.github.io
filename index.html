I received the bachelor's degree at 2017 from the Department of Automation, Northeastern University with honors.

Research interest

I am broadly interested in research on the representation and adaptation of artifical intelligence. I believe AI in the future should be reasonable, learn with little supervision, and learn continuously over space and time. I work with computer vision, mechine learning, and optimization.
